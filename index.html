dRT Philosophy

Vitalik Buterin might have just carried out a pretentious donation drive with all the dog-themed tokens to increase his wealth, and robbed the poor to give to the poorer. Unfortunately, such a huge market manipulation is being praised by the media. With dRT, we care for both - the poor and the poorer. dRT is all about the common people coming together to show their strength against the individual whales in the space. This is about the fundamental sentiment in the decentralised world - fighting the monopoly and market manipulation. We are in this together!

A fair community-first tokenomics

Our tokenomics puts community and humanity first:

  • Total supply: 100 billion dRT.
  • The dRT tokens represent voting rights on the platform. The voting will first go live in December 2021.
  • 90% of the token supply locked in Quickswap LP and the keys burnt for good! We decided to go with Quickswap (on L2 Polygon network) to avoid the unrealistic gas fees on Uniswap (on Ethereum network). Yes, we will not do any official listing on a CEX. We will remain fair and decentralised throughout the journey.
  • 6% to the team (dev+marketing).
  • 2% to donation reserve.
  • 2% for a possible future airdrop to some of the dog-meme holders who lost against the billionaire whale Vitalik Buterin. We are still evaluating which token (SHIB, ELON or AKITA) holders to consider for this.

    How does Doge Rescue give back to the community?

    We are the real donors! Every Christmas, based voting by the dRT holders on the blockchain, the community will decide where the tokens should be donated next year. 2% of the donation reserve (0.04% of total supply) or 40 million tokens (of 100 billion total supply) are donated per year (vested slowly over the year). The donation reserve at this rate can sustain the donations for next 50 years.
