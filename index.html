dRT Philosophy

It is pretty unfortunate to see the crypto markets being manipulated by a few large whales. However, it is even worse when these manipulations are labelled as donations and praised by others. Robbing the poor to give to the poorer can not be justified. With dRT, we care for both - the poor and the poorer. This is about the fundamental sentiment in the decentralised space - fighting the monopoly of a few influencial and rich entities. We are in this together!

A fair community-first tokenomics

Our tokenomics puts community and humanity first:

  • The dRT tokens represent voting rights on the platform. The voting will first go live in December 2021. In the future, you might also be able to exchange your tokens for unique non-fungible assets!
  • Total supply: 1 billion dRT.
  • 90% of the token supply locked in Quickswap LP forever! We decided to go with Quickswap (on L2 Polygon network) to avoid the unrealistic gas fees on Uniswap (on Ethereum network). Yes, we will not do any official listing on a CEX. We will remain fair and decentralised throughout the journey.
  • 6% to the team (dev+marketing).
  • 2% to donation reserve.
  • 2% for a possible future airdrop to some of the dog-meme holders who lost against the recent market manipulation by a popular whale. We are still evaluating which token (SHIB, ELON or AKITA) holders to consider for this. As far as we know, this would be the largest airdrop on the polygon network!

    How does Doge Rescue give back to the community?

    We are the real donors! Every Christmas, blockchain based voting by the dRT holders will decide where the tokens should be donated next year. 2% of the donation reserve (0.04% of total supply) or 400K tokens (of 1 billion total supply) are donated per year (vested slowly over the year). The donation reserve at this rate can sustain the donations for next 50 years.
